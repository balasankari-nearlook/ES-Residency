Kodaikanal

Nestled amidst the Western Ghats, Kodaikanal is a serene hill station renowned for its natural beauty and tranquil atmosphere. Explore the enchanting Kodaikanal Lake, stroll along the picturesque Coaker's Walk, and unwind amidst the lush greenery of Bryant Park.

Munnar

Welcome to Munnar, a paradise for nature lovers in the Western Ghats. Marvel at the verdant Tea Gardens Munnar, encounter exotic wildlife at Eravikulam National Park and soak in the breathtaking views from Top Station Munnar.

Vaigai Dam

Experience the tranquility of Vaigai Dam, a scenic marvel located in the heart of Theni. Enjoy a leisurely boat ride on the serene waters, capture stunning views from the Vaigai Dam viewpoint, and relax amidst the lush greenery surrounding Vaigai Dam Park.

Suruli Falls

Discover the natural beauty of Suruli Falls, a captivating waterfall nestled amidst the verdant hills of Theni. Embark on an adventurous trek to reach the falls, capture breathtaking photographs of the cascading waters, and enjoy a refreshing swim in the natural pool.

Meghamalai Wildlife Sanctuary

Immerse yourself in the wilderness of Meghamalai Wildlife Sanctuary, home to a diverse range of flora and fauna. Embark on an exhilarating safari to spot elusive wildlife species, trek through lush forests, and marvel at the vibrant birdlife that inhabits the sanctuary.

Sothuparai Dam

Experience the serenity of Sothuparai Dam, a tranquil retreat nestled amidst the scenic landscapes of Theni. Enjoy a peaceful boat ride on the calm waters, capture stunning sunset views, and savor a picnic amidst the lush greenery of the surrounding park.

Kumbakkarai Falls

Explore the majestic beauty of Kumbakkarai Falls, a breathtaking waterfall in the heart of Theni. Embark on an adventurous trek to reach the falls, take a refreshing swim in the crystal-clear waters, and marvel at the panoramic views from the nearby viewpoint.

Vellimalai

Discover the untouched beauty of Vellimalai, a scenic hill station offering breathtaking views of the surrounding valleys and forests. Embark on a thrilling trek to reach the summit, capture stunning sunrise views, and encounter diverse wildlife along the way.

Meghamalai Tea Estates

Indulge in the tranquility of Meghamalai Tea Estates, where vast stretches of tea plantations paint the landscape in shades of green. Take a guided tour of the estates, learn about the tea-making process, and savor the aroma of freshly brewed tea.

Thekkady Wildlife Sanctuary

Experience the wonders of Thekkady Wildlife Sanctuary, a biodiverse haven located in the heart of Theni. Embark on an unforgettable safari to spot majestic wildlife species, cruise along the scenic Periyar Lake, and explore the lush forests teeming with flora and fauna.
